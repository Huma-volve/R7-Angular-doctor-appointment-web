Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions ?
We’ve got Answers!

This app is used to help patients easily find and book appointments with doctors based on specialty, location, and availability. It allows users to search for nearby doctors, view their profiles, and schedule appointments quickly and conveniently.

Is the app free to use? Yes, the app is free to use. Patients can search for doctors, view profiles, and book appointments without any cost.

How can I find a doctor? You can find a doctor by using the search feature in the app. You can search by name, specialty, or location. You can also use the “Search by Location” button to automatically find nearby doctors based on your current position.

Can I cancel my appointment? Yes, you can cancel your appointment at any time. Simply go to your bookings page, select the appointment you want to cancel, and follow the cancellation steps.

What payment methods are supported? The app supports multiple payment methods, including cash, credit/debit cards, and online payment options depending on the doctor or clinic’s setup.

How do I contact customer support? You can contact customer support through the “Contact Us” section in the app. You can send a message directly, or use the provided email and phone number for assistance.
